FAQ

1. Are the Bonplats single-use/disposable?

Bonplats is intended for single-use, but our line is actually fully biodegradable and compostable. Because we do not use any bonding agents, lacquers or chemicals and our product is natural and porous, we cannot legally recommend washing and reusing. However, we can tell you that our customers often write in that they hand wash the plates to enjoy them and again.

2. Are Bonplats dishes completely non-toxic?

Yes! Our products are completely non-toxic because we only use leaves and water in the process.

3. Are Bonplats products clean? Do I need to wash them before I use them?

You can serve meals on Bonplats straight out of the package. All Bonplats dinnerware is cleaned and UV sterilized in our factory. so they’re extremely clean. However, there are some kinds of fragment on the products, so we recommend a swipe once before using.

4. Will Bonplats products affect the taste of my food?

No. Bonplats won’t alter the taste of your food.

5. Is it safe to put Bonplats in a microwave, oven, or fridge?

Yes. Bonplats is significantly more durable than plastic or paper plates. It’s perfectly safe to heat Bonplats in the microwave for up to 2 minutes on high or in an oven up to 350ºF degrees for 45 minutes. You can also store Bonplats in the refrigerator or freezer.

6. Are Bonplats dishes recyclable?

No. Recycling involves melting permanent materials (i.e. metals, glass, plastic, and etc), and reforming them for reuse. Since our products are made from leaves, they’re compostable and will naturally return to the earth in 2 months. This process uses 90% less energy than recycling.

7. Do Bonplats hold liquids?

Yes. liquids, soups and anything else you can cook up with Bonplats

8. How does Bonplats ensure its manufacturers adhere to human rights standards?

We require that the factories operate under sound labor conditions, pay fair wages and follow local labor standards. Bonplats requires code of conduct signed by all factories. Our production staff routinely visits these factories to ensure they are maintaining these working standards.

9. How strong are Bonplats?

Very strong. In fact, we are pretty sure they are the strongest disposables out there. They are suitable for liquids and hot food.

10. What are the prints on my Bonplats?

These are the natural marks and patterns found on the Bonplats are made from.

11. Did we miss any thing above ?

Please email us at help@bonplats.com. We’ll get back to you right away with an answer to your question.
